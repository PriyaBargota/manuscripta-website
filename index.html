About the Project

Manuscripta is an innovative sensory-friendly educational platform developed as part of the UCL Computer Science Systems Engineering module. Designed specifically for E-ink tablets, our platform reduces eye strain and visual overstimulation while providing an optimal learning environment for all students, including those with sensory sensitivities.

At the core of Manuscripta is our intelligent handwriting recognition system that converts handwritten notes into digital text, making learning more accessible and interactive. Combined with AI-powered content generation and real-time teacher monitoring, Manuscripta bridges the gap between traditional pen-and-paper learning and modern digital education.
