About the Project

Manuscripta is an innovative educational technology platform developed as part of the UCL Computer Science Systems Engineering module. The project aims to revolutionize how educational content is delivered to students using E-ink tablets, combining the benefits of digital technology with the reading comfort of paper.

Our platform leverages AI to generate personalized educational content, while providing teachers with powerful monitoring tools to track student progress in real-time. Built with modern technologies including Spring Boot, Android MVVM architecture, and Room database, Manuscripta represents a comprehensive solution to digital learning challenges.
